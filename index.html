About

Joe Miller

After college, I lived in Thailand and worked as a freelance SEO writer, fighting in the Thai combat sport of Muay Thai as much as possible. More on my fight life here. My freelance writing started to turn into graphic design at the request of my clients, which then began to turn into static web development. That's when I started to learn JavaScript. Upon returning to the US, my partner and I chose to move from Tallahassee, FL to NYC to chase big fights and big opportunities.


My graduation from Flatiron School's Immersive Software Engineering program in October makes it almost two years we have been getting lost in the subway system of New York City.
