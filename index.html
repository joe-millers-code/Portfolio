About

Joe Miller

I was born and raised right outside Ft. Lauderdale, FL. I started fighting competitively when I was 17. After college, I lived in Thailand as a freelance SEO writer and won the Thapae Stadium Championship, my first real championship belt as a professional. My writing started to turn into graphic design at the request of my clients, which then started to turn into the makings of web development. Upon returning to the US, my partner and I chose to move from Tallahassee, FL to NYC to chase big fights and big opportunities.


My graduation from Flatiron School's Immersive Software Engineering program in October makes it almost two years we have been getting lost in the subway system of New York City.
